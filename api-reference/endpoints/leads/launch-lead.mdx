---
openapi: post /leads/review/{leadId}
description: Manually launches (reviews) a single lead in its campaign.
---

Use this endpoint to launch a lead that is waiting for review, without enabling
campaign-wide auto-review.

It bypasses the campaign's `autoReview` setting and the email deliverability
filter — you are opting in for this specific lead — but still enforces every
other launch guard: the campaign must have no step errors, any AI variables
required by the campaign must be valid for the lead, and a sender must be
available.

This endpoint requires an `emailPro` plan or higher.
